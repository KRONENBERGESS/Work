Our Story

WHO WE ARE & WHAT WE STAND FOR

Our practice started with a brother and sister team who have decades of experience each. Over the years as our Practice grew, we brought on more dentists that whose skill level and dentistry practice is on par with our own.

We as a family, always prioritized giving back to the community. We work with universities so we can instruct interns on the best practices by shadowing us and provide a more personal learning experience. We want to train the dentists of the future who will be treating our children and yours!

We also focus on raising awareness for dental hygeiene. We have carried out several campaigns that inform people on different gum diseases, dental hygeiene practices focused on kids and adults. Our practice wants to do good
