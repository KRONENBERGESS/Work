Our packages

Pricing is based upon the season you're travelling.

  • We will handle your accomodations
  • Provide you with a local tour guide that will drive you around the cities included in the packages.
  • If you so require, we will include additional destinations not in the package for a fee.

Summer package: Summer season is a great time to visit Georgia, especially if you plan on traveling around. In this time period, you can go to mountainous areas for stunning views, walk inside historical canyons, take a dip in the sandy beaches of Ureki, swim in luxurious pools at resorts and enjoy the fresh air.

Winter package: Adrenaline lovers can skii down the pristine slopes of Gudauri. And with heli-skiing packages, you can be the alone on the slopes all day. You can also stay at Rooms Hotel Kazbegi in Stepantsminda town, not far from Gudauri, stunning views of Mount Kazbegi
